Classic Burger

Index

A juicy, seasoned beef patty served on a toasted bun with fresh vegetable toppings and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheddar cheese slides
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Form the beef into flat patties and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or pan-fry for 3 or 4 minutes per side; add cheese during the last minute to melt.
  3. Lightly toast the buns on the grill or in a pan.
  4. Spread the mustard and mayonnaise on a bun, add some ketchup, place the patty on the bottom bun and stack your toppings as high as you like

Green Enchiladas

Index

Soft corn tortillas stuffed with shredded chicken, smothered in a tangy tomatillo sauce, and topped with melted cheese and onion slices.

Ingredients

  • Shredded cooked chicken breast
  • Salsa verde
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded Monterey cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Onion slices

Steps

  1. Dip tortillas in warm salsa verde to soften them.
  2. Place chicken inside each tortilla, roll them up, and place in a baking dish.
  3. Pour the remaining salsa over the rolls and sprinkle generously with cheese.
  4. Heat at 350°F (175°C) for 15 minutes until the cheese bubbles.
  5. Add sour cream and fresh onion slices on top.

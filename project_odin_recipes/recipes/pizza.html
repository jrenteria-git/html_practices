Marghuerita Pizza

Index

The Marghuerita Pizza is not just a simple pizza, it is a cultural heritage. The edge of the pizza is high, puffy, and full of air, while the center is very thin.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Oil
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to its highest setting (typically 250°C - 275°C) with the grill function on top.
  2. Stretch the dough by hand on flour. Do not use a rolling pin (or you will kill the air bubbles in the edges). Leave the center thin and the edges thick.
  3. Place a pan over high heat. Place the stretched dough in the pan without oil. When the base is golden brown (2 minutes), turn off the heat.
  4. With the dough still in the pan, add the tomato, mozzarella, and oil.
  5. Place the pan (if the handle is suitable) or transfer the pizza to a baking sheet on the top rack of the oven. In 2 or 3 minutes, when the edge has puffed up and the cheese is bubbling, remove it from the oven. Add the basil as it comes out.
